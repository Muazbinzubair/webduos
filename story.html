Our Story

The Journey

We are two passionate students from LGS School who discovered our interest in computer sciences early on. Recognizing our potential, our teachers selected us to participate in a web development competition in 2024. We practiced consistently for 3 to 4 months—and when the day came, we proudly secured 1st position among 15 branches.

Our teachers and parents were incredibly proud of us, and it was at that moment we realized the potential of our skills. That's when we decided to seriously pursue web development. We committed to learning more, building our grip on this field, and helping others by creating websites at affordable prices—so our skills could grow while clients benefited too.

In 2025, during our summer holidays, we began working on real projects. Our work led us to successfully design and publish two e-commerce websites and two business websites. The site owners were extremely satisfied, and that motivated us even more. That’s when we officially started WebDuos—our digital identity. With dedication, passion, and a love for tech, we aim to keep growing, practicing, and delivering professional websites that truly help businesses thrive in today’s digital world.
